Methods of Elaboration

Background Info:
  • Past achievement
  • Personal goals
  • Membership in organizations
  • Origins (where are you from, etc.)
  • Hobbies and interests
Outcomes
  • What was accomplished?
  • Who won?
  • What goals were met?
Statistics
  • Height and weight (sports only!)
  • Scoring average, yards per game, etc.
  • Team record, winning streaks, district standing...
  • Personal records
  • In appropriate cases, GPA or class standing
  • Class schedule
Quotes
  • “Direct verbatim quotes,” senior Johnny Public said.
  • Paraphrasing, if done correctly
Generic
  • Platitudes about sport, subject, grade level, school policy, etc.
  • English was required all four years. Football demanded endurance...
